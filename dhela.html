Dhela Zone 🐅

A Serene Wildlife Experience in Corbett

Overview 🌿

Dhela Zone is one of the newest eco-tourism zones of Jim Corbett National Park. Situated in the buffer area, this zone is known for its diverse landscapes, peaceful surroundings, and growing wildlife population.

Dhela is an excellent choice for visitors looking for a less crowded and more relaxed safari experience while still enjoying rich biodiversity.

Why Choose Dhela Zone? 🐯

It beautifully preserves the raw, quiet ambiance of the jungle. Without the large crowds of the more prominent zones, you get an uninterrupted nature experience.

  • Less Crowded A much lower tourist density compared to the core zones.
  • Excellent Wildlife Sightings Good chances of spotting tigers, wild elephants, and leopards.
  • Diverse Terrain Rich mix of forests, grasslands, and scenic water bodies.
  • Ideal for Photography Quiet environments are perfect for capturing wildlife and nature.
  • Great for Everyone Highly suitable for both beginners and seasoned wildlife enthusiasts.

Safari Experience 🚙

Dhela Zone offers a comfortable and immersive jeep safari through scenic forest routes.

Jeep Safari

🟢 Primary Option

  • Up to 6 visitors per jeep
  • Available in morning & afternoon slots
  • Covers a wide buffer forest area
  • Guided by experienced forest guides

Major Attractions 🌅

🌳 Mixed Forest Landscape

The zone features a stunning combination of dense forests, open grasslands, and seasonal streams, providing excellent opportunities for highly diverse wildlife sightings.

🐘 Wildlife Sightings

Commonly spotted animals in Dhela include:

  • Royal Bengal Tiger 🐅
  • Asian Elephant 🐘
  • Leopard 🐆
  • Spotted Deer (Chital) 🦌
  • Sambar Deer & Wild Boar 🐗

🦅 Bird Watching

Dhela is home to a wide, beautiful variety of bird species, such as:

  • Indian Roller
  • Kingfisher
  • Crested Serpent Eagle
  • Peafowl (Indian Peacock) 🦚

🗓️ Best Time to Visit

  • Zone Open: Mid-October to end of June
  • Nov to Feb: Cool and pleasant weather
  • March to June: Higher chances of wildlife sightings

📍 Location & Access

  • Nearest Town: Ramnagar
  • Entry Gate: Dhela Gate
  • Distance: Approx. 20–25 km from Ramnagar

🎟️ Permits & Booking

  • Online permit booking is mandatory
  • Limited number of vehicles allowed per day
  • Valid government ID required
  • Advance booking is strongly recommended

⚠️ Visitor Guidelines

  • Plastic items are strictly prohibited
  • Loud noise and music are not allowed
  • Follow all instructions given by forest guides
  • Maintain a safe distance from wildlife

💡 Expert Travel Tips

  • Choose early morning safaris for significantly better wildlife activity.
  • Wear neutral-colored clothing (green, brown, beige).
  • Carry binoculars and a high-quality camera 📸.
  • Stay totally quiet to improve chances of animal sightings.

✨ WHAT MAKES DHELA UNIQUE?

A Private Wilderness

Dhela Zone stands out for its peaceful atmosphere and lower tourist density, offering a more private and undisturbed wildlife experience. It is perfect for those who want to explore Corbett beyond the more crowded zones.
