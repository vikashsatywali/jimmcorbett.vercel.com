Dhikala Zone 🐅

The Ultimate Wildlife Experience in Jim Corbett

Overview 🌿

Dhikala Zone is the largest and most popular zone of Jim Corbett National Park. Known for its vast grasslands, dense forests, and proximity to the Ramganga River, Dhikala offers the most authentic and immersive jungle experience in India.

It is the only zone where visitors can stay deep inside the forest, making it a top choice for wildlife enthusiasts, photographers, and adventure seekers.

Why Choose Dhikala Zone? 🐯

Dhikala stands apart as the premier destination for a true wilderness experience. The combination of dense canopy, open grasslands, and abundant water sources creates the perfect habitat for a diverse range of wildlife.

  • Highest Probability of Spotting Tigers Known as the prime territory for the majestic Royal Bengal Tiger.
  • Expansive Grasslands (Chaurs) Ideal for unobstructed, wide-angle wildlife sightings.
  • Ramganga River Views Breathtaking scenic views and an active watering hole for wildlife.
  • Rich Biodiversity Abundant elephants, deer, crocodiles, and exotic bird species.
  • Exclusive Forest Stay The only zone offering night stays deep inside the core park area.

Safari Experience 🚙

A safari in Dhikala is not just a ride — it's a deep exploration of the wild.

1. Canter Safari

🟢 Most Recommended for Day Visitors

  • Open bus with 16–18 seats
  • Highly budget-friendly option
  • Operates on fixed routes and timings
  • Ideal for groups and individual travelers

2. Jeep Safari

🟢 Limited Access

  • Private, highly flexible experience
  • Covers deeper, secluded forest areas
  • Note: Only available for guests staying inside the Dhikala Forest Lodge
  • Requires advance booking

Stay at Dhikala Forest Lodge 🏨

Dhikala Forest Lodge offers a rare and magical opportunity to stay inside the core forest area, waking up to the sounds of the jungle.

  • Located amidst dense wilderness 🌲
  • Watchtower for wildlife observation 🐘
  • Simple yet highly comfortable accommodation
  • Unmatched authentic jungle atmosphere

Important Note ⚠️

Accommodation is extremely limited and must be booked well in advance (ideally 45-90 days prior to your visit).

Major Attractions 🌅

🌾 Dhikala Grasslands (Chaur)

A vast, stunning open landscape famous for frequent tiger and wild elephant sightings. It offers incredible photography opportunities.

🌊 Ramganga Reservoir

A major water source cutting through the zone, attracting a huge population of crocodiles, elephants, and migratory birds.

🦅 Bird Watching

Home to over 500 species of birds, making it a paradise for birders. Look out for the Crested Serpent Eagle, Kingfishers, and Woodpeckers.

🗓️ Best Time to Visit

  • Zone Open: 15 November to 15 June
  • Nov to Feb: Pleasant weather, great scenery
  • March to June: Best time for tiger sightings

📍 Location & Access

  • Nearest Town: Ramnagar
  • Entry Gate: Dhangarhi Gate
  • Distance: Approx. 30 km deep inside from Ramnagar

🎟️ Permits & Booking

  • Online booking is strictly mandatory
  • Valid ID proof required for all visitors
  • Limited daily entries to preserve nature
  • Advance booking (30–45 days) recommended

⚠️ Visitor Guidelines

  • Plastic items are completely prohibited
  • Loud noise and music are not allowed
  • Follow instructions of forest guides
  • Carry valid ID at all times

💡 Expert Travel Tips

  • Choose early morning safaris for significantly better wildlife sightings.
  • Wear neutral-colored clothing (green, brown, beige) to blend in.
  • Carry a good camera and binoculars 📸 for distant sightings.
  • Maintain absolute silence to avoid disturbing the wildlife.
