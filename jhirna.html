Jhirna Zone 🐅

The Year-Round Safari Destination of Corbett

Overview 🌿

Jhirna Zone is one of the most accessible and unique zones of Jim Corbett National Park. Located in the southern part of the park, Jhirna is famous for being open throughout the year, unlike most other zones.

With its dry deciduous forests, open landscapes, and consistent wildlife movement, Jhirna offers a reliable safari experience in every season.

Why Choose Jhirna Zone? 🐯

Unlike other zones that close during monsoons, Jhirna remains operational, offering nature lovers a chance to see the jungle in its most lush, vibrant state.

  • Open All Year Round Subject to extreme weather conditions, you can visit Jhirna anytime.
  • Frequent Sloth Bear Sightings The dry terrain provides great opportunities to see tigers and sloth bears.
  • Less Crowded Enjoy a more peaceful and immersive jungle experience away from the rush.
  • Ideal for Off-Season Visits The best choice for monsoon and late-summer wildlife trips.
  • Rich Wildlife & Bird Variety A fantastic spot for birdwatchers and herbivore sightings.

Safari Experience 🚙

Jhirna Zone offers a smooth and enjoyable jeep safari experience through diverse forest terrain.

Jeep Safari

🟢 Main Option

  • 6 people per jeep
  • Covers wide forest areas
  • Morning and afternoon safari slots
  • Great for wildlife photography

Major Attractions 🌅

🌳 Dry Forest Landscape

Jhirna features a unique mix of dry deciduous forest and open grass patches, making wildlife tracking incredibly easier and more visible.

🐻 Sloth Bear Sightings

This zone is especially known and loved for frequent sloth bear sightings, an animal which is quite rare to spot in other zones.

🐘 Wildlife Sightings

Animals commonly seen in the Jhirna Zone:

  • Royal Bengal Tiger 🐅
  • Sloth Bear 🐻
  • Wild Elephants 🐘
  • Spotted Deer (Chital)
  • Nilgai (Blue Bull) & Wild Boar

🦅 Bird Watching

Jhirna is home to a fantastic array of bird species such as:

  • Indian Roller
  • Crested Serpent Eagle
  • Peafowl (Peacock)
  • Colorful Parakeets

🗓️ Best Time to Visit

  • Zone Open: Throughout the year
  • Nov to Feb: Pleasant winter weather
  • March to June: Higher chances of tiger sightings

📍 Location & Access

  • Nearest Town: Ramnagar
  • Entry Gate: Dhela Gate
  • Distance: Approx. 15–20 km from Ramnagar

🎟️ Permits & Booking

  • Online booking strictly mandatory
  • Limited jeep entries per day
  • Valid ID proof required for check-in
  • Advance booking recommended

⚠️ Visitor Guidelines

  • No plastic items allowed
  • Do not make loud noises
  • Follow forest rules and guide instructions
  • Respect wildlife and maintain distance

💡 Expert Travel Tips

  • Early morning safaris are the absolute best for wildlife sightings.
  • Wear neutral-colored clothes (green, brown, beige) so you blend in.
  • Carry binoculars and a good camera 📸 for optimal viewing.
  • Avoid sudden movements or noise to prevent scaring animals.

✨ WHAT MAKES JHIRNA UNIQUE?

A Reliable Safari Destination

Jhirna Zone stands out because it is open all year, making it the perfect option when other zones are closed during monsoon. Its consistent wildlife sightings and peaceful environment make it a favorite among regular visitors.
