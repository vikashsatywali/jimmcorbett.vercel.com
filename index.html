Best Time To Visit

Jim Corbett experiences three distinct seasons, each offering a uniquely mesmerizing vibe. Most safari zones remain open from mid-November to mid-June.

Winter (Nov - Feb)

Best for Wildlife spotting! The weather is pleasant and animals come out to bask in the sun.

Summer (Mar - Jun)

Ideal for Tiger sighting! The vegetation dries up, and wildlife frequently visits water bodies.

Monsoon (Jul - Oct)

For Nature Lovers. Core zones are closed, but buffer zones remain open. Lush greenery is at its peak.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries regarding safari bookings, resort stays, and park policies.

Safari booking kaise kare? (How to book a safari?)

Safari bookings can be made directly through our online portal. Select your date, preferred zone, and provide the ID proofs of all passengers. Advance booking (up to 45 days) is highly recommended.

ID proof kya chahiye? (What ID proof is required?)

Indian nationals can use Aadhar Card, Voter ID, Driving License, or Passport. Foreign nationals must provide their original Passport details. The same original ID must be carried during the safari.

Are kids allowed on the safari?

Yes, children of all ages are allowed. Children up to 5 years of age are free of charge. Full booking charges apply for older children.

What is your refund policy?

As per forest department rules, safari permits are completely non-refundable and non-transferable once booked. Resort cancellations are subject to the respective hotel's policy.
