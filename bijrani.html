Bijrani Zone 🐅

The Most Popular & Accessible Safari Zone of Corbett

Overview 🌿

Bijrani Zone is one of the most visited and easily accessible zones of Jim Corbett National Park. Located close to the main entry gate, Bijrani is known for its rich wildlife population, dense sal forests, and open grasslands.

This zone offers a perfect balance of accessibility and high wildlife sightings, making it ideal for both first-time visitors and experienced wildlife lovers.

Why Choose Bijrani Zone? 🐯

Known for its diverse flora and highly active fauna, Bijrani is incredibly rewarding. With varied landscapes, every twist and turn reveals something beautiful.

  • High Chances of Royal Bengal Tiger Frequently reported tiger sightings in this zone.
  • Rich Biodiversity Spot large herds of elephants, varied deer species, and exotic birds.
  • Beautiful Landscapes A stunning mix of dense, cool forests and bright, open grasslands.
  • Easy Access Extremely convenient location directly from Ramnagar town.
  • Perfect for Short Safaris Provides a comfortable, action-packed experience without long travel times.

Safari Experience 🚙

Bijrani Zone offers one of the absolute best jeep safari experiences in Corbett.

Jeep Safari

🟢 Primary Option

  • 6 people per jeep
  • Personalized and flexible experience
  • Morning and evening safari slots
  • Ideal for wildlife photography

Major Attractions 🌅

🌾 Bijrani Grassland

A wide open area where animals are often spotted grazing — incredibly great for natural lighting and photography.

🌳 Dense Sal Forest

The thick forest canopy provides a cool natural habitat for tigers, elephants, and leopards seeking shelter.

🐘 Wildlife Sightings

Common animals frequently seen wandering in Bijrani:

  • Tigers 🐅
  • Asian Elephants 🐘
  • Spotted Deer (Chital)
  • Sambar Deer & Wild Boar

🦅 Bird Watching

Bijrani is a serene paradise for bird lovers, featuring species like:

  • Crested Serpent Eagle
  • Vibrant Parakeets
  • Woodpeckers
  • Drongos

🗓️ Best Time to Visit

  • Zone Open: 15 October to 30 June
  • Oct to Feb: Cool and highly pleasant
  • March to June: Best for wildlife sightings

📍 Location & Access

  • Nearest Town: Ramnagar
  • Entry Gate: Amdanda Gate
  • Distance: Approx. 8–10 km from Ramnagar

🎟️ Permits & Booking

  • Online booking is strictly mandatory
  • Limited jeeps allowed per day
  • ID proof is completely mandatory
  • Advance booking highly recommended

⚠️ Visitor Guidelines

  • No plastic allowed
  • Avoid loud noise and disturbance
  • Follow guide instructions
  • Maintain safe distance from animals

💡 Expert Travel Tips

  • Early morning safaris always offer better chances of active sightings.
  • Wear earth-toned clothes (green, brown, beige) to remain inconspicuous.
  • Carry a camera with a good zoom lens 📸 for distant subjects.
  • Stay completely silent for a more immersive wildlife experience.

✨ WHY BIJRANI IS SPECIAL?

A Quick Yet Thrilling Safari

Bijrani Zone is perfect if you want a quick yet thrilling jungle safari without going too deep into the forest like Dhikala. Its easy accessibility and rich wildlife make it one of the top safari choices in Corbett.
