Garjiya Zone 🐅

A Unique Blend of Wildlife & Spirituality

🙏 Garjiya Devi Temple
A Sacred Highlight

Dedicated to Goddess Parvati, the temple is uniquely situated on a large rock in the middle of the beautiful Kosi River.

  • One of the most famous temples near Corbett
  • Offers stunning river views 🌊
  • Peaceful spiritual atmosphere
  • Major attraction during Kartik Purnima

Travel Tip

Many visitors beautifully combine a temple darshan + jungle safari in a single, memorable trip!

Why Choose Garjiya Zone? 🐯

Offering a refreshing mix of culture, convenience, and nature, this zone is highly accessible and perfectly suited for those short on time.

  • Closest Safari Zone Extremely convenient to reach from Ramnagar.
  • Wildlife + Spirituality A highly unique combination you won't find in any other zone.
  • Less Crowded Enjoy your safari in a far quieter environment than core zones.
  • Scenic Landscapes Breath-taking riverbelts interwoven with dense forests.
  • Ideal for Short Trips Perfectly optimized for highly convenient, fulfilling quick visits.

Safari Experience 🚙

Garjiya offers a smooth and highly accessible jeep safari that is perfect for beginners and families.

Jeep Safari

🟢 Primary Option

  • Up to 6 people per jeep
  • Morning & afternoon slots available
  • Guided by trained forest experts
  • Covers mixed terrain (forest + river)

Major Attractions 🌅

🌊 Kosi River Belt

The nearby majestic Kosi river consistently attracts animals searching for water, especially making early morning and evening safaris highly rewarding.

🌳 Forest Landscape

A pristine blend of dense forest canopies and wide open grass areas, perfectly ideal for spotting an array of wildlife safely.

🐘 Wildlife Sightings

Common animals in the Garjiya Zone:

  • Royal Bengal Tiger 🐅
  • Asian Elephant 🐘
  • Leopard 🐆
  • Spotted Deer (Chital) 🦌
  • Sambar Deer & Wild Boar 🐗

🦅 Bird Watching

Garjiya is also fantastic for avid bird lovers. Look out for:

  • Kingfisher
  • Indian Roller
  • Crested Serpent Eagle
  • Peafowl (Indian Peacock) 🦚

🗓️ Best Time to Visit

  • Zone Open: 15 October to 30 June
  • Winter (Nov-Feb): Pleasant weather 🌤️
  • Summer (Mar-Jun): Better wildlife sightings 🔥

📍 Location & Access

  • Nearest Town: Ramnagar
  • Entry Gate: Garjiya Gate
  • Distance: Approx. 9–12 km

🎟️ Permits & Booking

  • Online booking strictly required
  • Limited vehicles permitted per day
  • Valid ID proof is mandatory
  • Advance booking highly recommended

⚠️ Visitor Guidelines

  • No plastic items allowed at all
  • Avoid making loud noise
  • Always follow guide instructions
  • Respect wildlife and temple surroundings

💡 Expert Travel Tips

  • Combine temple visit + safari for a full, rich experience.
  • Visit the temple early in the morning to enjoy less crowd.
  • Wear comfortable footwear (there are stairs at the temple).
  • Carry a camera & binoculars 📸 for the safari.

✨ WHAT MAKES GARJIYA TRULY UNIQUE?

Nature Meets Spirituality

Garjiya Zone is the only place in Corbett where you can experience both spirituality and wildlife together. From the peaceful vibes of the temple to the thrill of a jungle safari, it offers a complete and diverse travel experience.
