How to Choose the Best Zone?

  • Tiger Sightings: Prefer Dhikala or Bijrani
  • Budget Safari: Jhirna or Dhela
  • Bird Watching: Durga Devi or Sitabani
  • Peace & Quiet: Dhela or Garjiya

Best Time to Visit

  • Winter (Nov–Feb): Pleasant weather & tremendous bird watching.
  • Summer (Mar–Jun): Optimal season for active tiger sightings.
  • Monsoon (Jul–Oct): Main core closed, only Jhirna & Dhela open.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I visit the Dhikala zone on a Jeep Safari if I am not staying there?

No. The Dhikala core zone is strictly reserved for tourists staying at the Dhikala Forest Rest House for Jeep Safaris. Day visitors can only explore Dhikala via the Canter Safari organized by the forest department.

How far in advance should I book my safari zone?

Permits for Jim Corbett open 45 days in advance and get sold out within minutes, especially for peak seasons and popular zones like Dhikala and Bijrani. We highly recommend booking at least 60 days in advance to secure your spot.

Can I shift from one zone to another during my safari?

No, once a safari permit is issued for a specific zone, it is non-transferable and non-refundable. Vehicles are strictly tracked at their respective entry gates, and zone-swapping is strictly prohibited by forest authorities.
