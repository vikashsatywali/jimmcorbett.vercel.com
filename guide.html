Jim Corbett National Park is a globally renowned sanctuary where untamed wilderness meets majestic landscapes. Nestled deeply within the sub-Himalayan belt, it offers an escape defined by crisp mountain air, the elusive roar of the Bengal tiger, and the tranquil flow of the Kosi and Ramganga rivers.
