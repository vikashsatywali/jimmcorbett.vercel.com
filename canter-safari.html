Canter Safari Adventure

Experience the rare thrill of Dhikala's wildlife in a shared open-roof journey.

A Canter Safari in Jim Corbett National Park is the most economical and exciting way to explore the world-famous Dhikala zone. Ride through dense forests, golden grasslands, and dried riverbeds in a 16-seater open bus and witness the raw beauty of nature. Whether you're a wildlife enthusiast or traveling in a large group, a canter safari offers an unforgettable jungle experience.

What is Canter Safari? 🌿

Canter Safari is a guided wildlife tour conducted in an open 16-seater bus, operated strictly by the forest department.

It allows day-visitors to explore the deep forest areas of the Dhikala zone—the core area famous for the highest tiger density—which is otherwise inaccessible for non-staying guests.

Unlike jeep safari, canter safari is a highly structured group experience. It is less private but extremely budget-friendly and arguably the absolute best way for non-residential tourists to access the legendary Dhikala grasslands.

Why Choose Canter Safari? 🐅

The elevated seating of the canter bus provides a unique vantage point for spotting wildlife in tall grasses. Here's why it's a popular choice:

  • Dhikala Zone Access The only way for day-visitors to explore the iconic core Dhikala zone.
  • Ideal for Groups Perfect for large extended families, corporate groups, and budget travelers.
  • Elevated Photography The height of the canter offers an excellent top-down angle for capturing stunning wildlife photography over the high grass.

Safari Zones Covered 🗺️

Canter Safari is exclusively designed to navigate the vast expanses of the world-renowned Dhikala zone.

"Dhikala is the beating heart of Corbett. Its vast 'Chaur' (grasslands) and the Ramganga reservoir make it the ultimate haven for the Bengal Tiger."

Dhikala Zone

The most iconic and famous zone. Loved for its massive grasslands and exceptionally high tiger sighting percentages.

Wildlife Spotting

Known not just for tigers, but for massive herds of Asian Elephants, diverse deer species, and crocodiles by the riverbed.

Best Time To Visit 🌅

Nov to June: Dhikala is open.
March to June: Best chances to spot tigers.
Winter: Pleasant, great bird watching.

Safari Timings

Canter Safari operates precisely in two major shifts daily starting from Ramnagar. Timing slightly varies based on season.

Morning: 6:00 AM – 11:30 AM
Evening: 12:00 PM – 5:30 PM

Essential Booking Information 📋

Everything you need to know before securing your permit.

Required Documents

You must have proper documentation to enter the forest reserve:

  • Valid Government ID: Aadhar Card, Passport, or Driving License.
  • Visitor Details: Accurate Name, age, and gender of all joining members.

How to Book?

Booking procedures are strictly regulated:

  1. Select Dhikala zone & preferred time (AM/PM).
  2. Provide ID details online.
  3. Make full advance payment.
  4. Receive official confirmation slip.

Important Guidelines

  • Always follow the guide's instructions.
  • Do not ever get down from the canter bus.
  • Maintain total silence in the forest.
  • Avoid plastic. Respect the wilderness.
